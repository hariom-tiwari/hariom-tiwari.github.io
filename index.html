Hachibi and Kyuubi with their Jinchuriki

Killer Bee is the Jinchuriki of the Hachibi-the beast whose head is of a bull and body of an octopus. It's special power is using bijuu bombs. Naruto is the Jinchuriki of the Kyuubi. Kyuubi can use ying and yang releases. He is said to be the strongest tailed beast in the Ninja world.

The jutsu that Naruto can use are:

  • Shadow Clone Jutsu
  • Sexy Jutsu
  • Summoning Jutsu
  • Rasengan
  • Rasen-Shuriken
  • Tailed-Beast Bomb
